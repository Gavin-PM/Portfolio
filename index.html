Musical Composition with Piano and Sax

This project combines my interest in Musical Composition, Alto Saxophone, and the Piano into an adaptation of the piece, "Fly Me to the Moon".

Originally composed by Frank Sinatra, I added more rhythms and added my own spin onto the song. In total, it consists of 1 piano and 3 saxophones playing.

These were all recorded in different sessions, and pieced together in premiere pro using my score.

Playing, Editing, and Composition: Gavin
Software: MuseScore, Adobe Premiere Pro
Bass Clef of Piano: Public Domain, Valdinei Silva Papa
Filming: Zhonggui Zhao (my dad)

The "Daily Recap" for Homeroom

Please Click on the Image to the right to see more.

"The Daily Recap" is a project that serves as a reminder to all of our classmates. It contains homework and reminders that we'd like to inform our classmates of.

Since the first issue (nearly 2 years ago), we have done more than 150 versions of the Daily Recap.

Mostly, it is completed by Afra Miao and I (roughly half participation each). Sometimes, Lucas Yang also helps with the design.

Editors: Afra Miao, Gavin Zhao, Lucas Yang
Other Contributors: Everyone in our class (8W)
